How to Use:

Input the desired meal and it will rank them based on how close they meet your nutritional needs based on your physical attributes (weight, height, sex, etc.).

The meals can come from any UCI restaurant, meaning you can see your macros from your favorite places on campus such as BrandyWine (a place people stay too often).
